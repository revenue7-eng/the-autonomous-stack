--- layout: default title: "Autonomy Map" nav_order: 3 --- # Autonomy Map Every technology in the catalog, plotted by **Autonomy** (horizontal) and **Transparency** (vertical). Click any dot to see its card.
--- ## Reading the map **Top-right corner (A3/T2)** is the ideal: fully autonomous, fully transparent. Most self-hosted open-source tools cluster here. **Bottom-left corner (A0/T0)** is the opposite: cloud-bound and closed. Google Drive, Notion. **The interesting space is in between.** Tailscale (A2/T1) is convenient but depends on a coordination server. Obsidian (A2/T0) stores your data locally but the app itself is closed. Plex (A1/T0) requires cloud authentication for your own media. The map makes conscious choice possible. Not every tool needs to be A3/T2. But every choice should be intentional. --- Explore the full [Technology Catalog](catalog/) or [run the audit](audit.html) on your own stack.
